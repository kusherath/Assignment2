About Us

Bake&Cake cake Shop is the creation of J.V.S. Pandithasekera (a.k.a: Akantha). Having been a top pastry chef for several years in the hotel industry, his passion to bring forth the rich and tasteful delights to the public drew him to start up his own restaurant. What started in 1985 as a small restaurant, “Sponge” is now an established and well known name among everyone.

With a dedicated and friendly staff of over 65 men and women, we strive to provide the best quality food and service always. Our menu currently consists of a diverse selection from sweets, cakes & savories to burgers, rice & noodles among many others.

A wide variety of delicious cakes, gateaux and sweet treats - from the all-time favorites to the trendy new comers, prepared with the finest ingredients.

Our scrumptious cup cakes are available in Chocolate, Vanilla and Red Velvet flavors . Customized design orders are accepted as well.

Comprising of a skilled and artistic staff, we accept orders for wedding structures, design cakes, shape cakes and even customized & personalized sponge creations.

Our dynamic catering team is equipped and ready to server any occasion. With the flexibility to provide you with a customized menu, we ensure our service blends smoothly with any event.

From birthday and wedding cakes to simple desserts and sweet treats, our delectable range cater to any occasion.

With the ability to customize your own menu, our catering team always deliver delicious food that will capture your imagination and exceed expectations.

Place your order for delivery or take-away with our fast and secure online ordering feature.
